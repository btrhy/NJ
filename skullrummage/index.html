What is it?

Skullrummage is a physical journey through Melbourne, while also an imaginative journey through the creative process



There are things to do at stops as well as the walks inbetween

@ @
@ @

What to bring!

You will only need 4 things.

1. A project needing ideas
2. Your phone fully charged
3. A physical sketchpad
4. Earphones or headphones

Starting

To start rummaging, get your skull on over to the first location and press the play button.

Listen to the audio and write or draw ideas in your sketchpad.

When you are ready to move on, press 'move on'

Stops & Walks

Upon completing the first stop, you will have to use your compass for the first time.

Simply follow the arrow until the distance reaches 0m
