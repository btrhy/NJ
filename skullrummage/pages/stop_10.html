Transcript

The last location. This brick wall is the final stop. Think of it like a facebook wall. but a wall of good ideas! Hidden at either end of the wall in amongst the plants is chalk. Your goal is to use all the skills you have learnt so far and come up with a solution to a world issue. This could be world hunger, racism or anything else. Use techniques like thinking big, music, random words and pretending. Or don’t, just write something smart that comes straight into your noggin.

One can create for personal enjoyment and that is OK. But one of the beauties of putting work out there to the world is the impact it can have on other people. You can change the world and its people, or just one persons world.

Well we are at the end now, a final congratulations to you. If you didn’t know already we are just about 50 meters from where we started so it will be easy to get back home. You have been wonderful so far and i wish you all the best, with your project. Thanks for coming on a skullrummage. You stay creative sandeigo!

Objectives

Find the chalk hidden in the plants at the end of the wall

Write a solution to a world issue on the wall
