How long will it take?

A skullrummage session depends on how long you spend at stops. On average we have found it takes people 2 and a bit hours without rushing. But you can skip parts you dont like, or come back to it.


Why is the website broken?

The site is broke, and so is the developer. He's just a university student learning to web dev and design, but he isn't perfect, far from it actually.


How do i see and save my progress?

Currently there is no way to save progress. But like old video games it is made easy to return to the same point as before. Simply go to the locations in the menu and filter and or scroll to find the one you were up to. Click it and wham! You can see where you are up to now in the same place.

What is it?

Skullrummage is a physical journey through Melbourne, while also an imaginative journey through the creative process. There are things to do at stops as well as the walks inbetween

Starting

To start rummaging, get your skull on over to the first location and press the play button.

Listen to the audio and write or draw ideas in your sketchpad.

When you are ready to move on, press 'move on'
